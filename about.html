About Trea

Educator & Academic Manager specializing in Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS), dedicated to transforming early childhood education.

My Journey in Education

With years of hands-on experience in early childhood education, I've dedicated my career to bridging the gap between pedagogical theory and practical classroom implementation.

My work encompasses teacher training, parent orientation, preschool branding, and student counseling—all grounded in the proven principles of EYFS.

I believe every child deserves a strong foundation, every parent deserves clear guidance, and every educator deserves proper training to make this possible.

Core Expertise Areas

EYFS Specialist

Teacher Trainer

Certifying educators in NTT, MTT, and modern teaching methodologies.

Child Counselor

Supporting emotional and behavioral development with empathy and expertise.

My Mission
