Frequently Asked Questions

Yes, all our certifications are widely recognized in the early childhood education sector and can be verified by educational institutions.

Do you provide placement assistance?

We offer placement assistance to all our certified graduates through our network of partner schools and preschools.

Can I take multiple programs together?

Yes, we offer combined programs with special discounts for students enrolling in multiple courses.

Is online training available?

We offer both in-person and hybrid training options. Some programs are available fully online with live interactive sessions.
